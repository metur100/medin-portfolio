Monthee Adventure

A 2D Platformer with RPG Elements – Brave the Wild Adventure!

Monthee Adventure is a 2D platformer with RPG elements, featuring Red Head, a fearless knight embarking on a wild adventure. Built for PC and Android devices, the game allows players to explore a colorful world, interact with NPCs, and solve puzzles while battling enemies and leveling up.

Game Highlights:

  • Action-Packed Platforming: Navigate the world by jumping, dashing, and using various abilities. Combat enemies with fireballs or break surprise boxes for hidden rewards.
  • Quests and Stories: Engage in side quests and mini-stories as you interact with NPCs. Helping those in need opens new adventures and storylines.
  • Items and Spells: Collect potions, spells, and special items to enhance your skills and overcome obstacles.
  • Leveling System: Gain experience points and level up your character, unlocking new abilities and increasing stats.
  • Puzzle Platforming: Solve small puzzles to progress through levels, engaging with a vibrant, interactive environment.
  • Unique NPCs: Meet quirky characters with distinctive stories and personalities that enrich the game world.
  • Cross-Platform: Enjoy Monthee Adventure on both PC and Android devices with seamless performance and responsive controls.

Technical Specifications:

  • Engine: Unity – leveraging free assets from the Unity Asset Store, with backgrounds from Craftpix and sound effects from Freesound.
  • Platforms: Windows and Android, designed for keyboard and mouse input.
  • Development Tools: Adobe After Effects for animations, Audacity for sound editing, and Unity for game development.
  • Development Time: 5 months – part-time solo development.
  • Language: English, with interactive tutorials for accessibility.

How It Works:

  • Start Your Adventure: Players begin by selecting a new game or continuing from a previous save. The game world gradually expands as quests and levels are unlocked.
  • Explore and Interact: Navigate through different levels, interact with NPCs, and uncover hidden paths by solving puzzles and completing side quests.
  • Combat and Skills: Engage enemies using fireballs and melee attacks. Unlock new skills and abilities by leveling up your character through experience gained in battles.
  • Collect and Equip: Gather potions, spells, and items from enemies, chests, and merchants. Equip gear to enhance your stats and abilities.
  • Quest Progression: Follow the main questline or engage in side quests that offer unique rewards and unlock new areas of the game world.
  • Level Completion: Complete each level by solving puzzles, defeating bosses, and meeting quest objectives. Advance to new stages and continue the journey.
  • Cross-Platform Play: Download and play on PC or Android devices. Save progress and switch between platforms for a seamless experience.
