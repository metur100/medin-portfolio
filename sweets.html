Sweets aus aller Welt

A promotional registration site for the first 100 enthusiasts

Dubai Chocolate presents a simple registration portal aimed at welcoming the first 100 customers. It allows visitors to submit their information to secure early access to exclusive offers or premium products.

Key Features:

  • Registration Form: Users can fill in their details to register.
  • First-100 Limit: Registration is limited to the first 100 submissions.
  • Email Collection: Emails are captured for follow-ups or access credentials.
  • Accessible Design: Works smoothly across devices.
  • Simple Interface: Minimalist design to reduce friction.

Technical Specifications:

  • Frontend: Plain HTML, CSS, maybe a little JavaScript for form validation.
  • Hosting: Static site hosted via GitHub Pages (or similar).
  • Data Handling: Form submissions go to a backend or service (could be email / database).
  • Design Aesthetic: Clean, simple typography, focused on user action.
